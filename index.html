About

Engineering structures for a more resilient future.

I am a civil engineer specializing in earthquake engineering and structural analysis. My research interests include structural dynamics, seismic risk assessment, seismic performance of RC and masonry buildings, computational modelling and machine-learning applications in civil engineering.

At the Institute of Himalayan Risk Reduction, I contribute to “Seismic Synergy: Learning From Traditional Wisdom To Bring A Resilient Future” , supporting seismic vulnerability assessment and heritage conservation of traditional Newari masonry buildings in the Kathmandu Valley.
